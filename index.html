EDUCATIONAL

There is no denying that the Internet has dramatically changed the state of education over the past 20 years or so. It brings primary sources into every classroom and allows for more open and rapid communication between teachers and students, among other things. But we have only seen the beginning. The way to reach out to a great future still has to be thought about.

E-COMMERCE

Before the introduction of the Internet, buying and selling of goods was done only physically. Ecommerce is the digital iteration of the traditional marketplace, and it has opened the world to a brand-new frontier in marketing efforts. E-commerce simply means trading of goods and currency via worldwide web.

ENTERTAINMENT

The future of digital entertainment therefore lies in its delivery. The Internet allows consumers to access just about any content at any time. Hardware manufacturers and software designers will continue to come up with novel ways for consumers to get the content they want. Gaming and Media Comsumption are the major entertainments that people seek in this modern world.

NEWS

Journalism originated as the reportage of current events, specifically in printed form, i.e. newspapers. However, propelled by the massive growth in technology and electronic communication over the past decade because of Internet, its scope has expanded and its defiinition has evolved into an all-encompassing domain of information.
